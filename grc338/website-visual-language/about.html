Welcome to The Lucky Cow, where happiness, animal welfare, and high-quality ice cream are the key ingredients of our delicious treats. Our mission is to bring joy to your taste buds while making a positive impact on the world. We believe in the power of sharing a sweet treat with someone you love, and that is why we strive to create a multigenerational gathering place where everyone feels welcome.

At The Lucky Cow, we are committed to using only the finest, freshest, and most creative local flavors to craft our outstanding ice cream. We partner with local farmers and producers who share our passion for sustainable and ethical practices, from beekeepers and jam-makers to strawberry farmers and coffee roasters. Our team works hard to ensure that every scoop of ice cream we serve is bursting with flavor and made with care.

We are also committed to being the best employer in our industry. We provide free health care, paid family leave, sick leave, and living wages to our employees. We believe that by creating a sustainable, enjoyable workplace, we can help build a healthier and more robust workforce. We are proud to demonstrate that an environmentally and socially responsible business can be profitable and continue to grow.

Our values are not only reflected in how we choose our farm partners and treat our employees but also in our giving program and social justice work. We give 1% of our sales each year to local non-profits and work hard to fight hunger through food bank donations and our own nonprofit, the Lucky Cow Milk Fund. We prioritize partnering with women, artists, and chefs of color in our collaborations and support organizations in our community that promote equity and social justice.

Ice cream is more than just a dessert. It's a way to connect with others, support local farmers and producers, and make a positive impact on the world. Come visit us and experience the joy of ethical, delicious, and delightful ice cream, from kitchen to cone.
